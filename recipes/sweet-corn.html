Sweet Corn

Description

Masala Corn is a healthy, quick and easy to prepare snack. It is made from boiled Sweet Corn kernels by adding butter, lemon juice and a few spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Sweet Corn Kernels
  • 2 Teaspoon Butter
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Chaat Masala
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Red Chili Powder
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Black Pepper Powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt or as per taste
  • 4-5 Sprigs of Coriander Leaves
  • 2 Teaspoon Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Boil the corn kernels in water for 6-7 minutes or until they become soft.
  2. Drain the water from the corn. Then melt some butter in a pan.
  3. Add the boiled corn kernels to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat till the butter is melted.
  4. Transfer the buttered corn to a mixing bowl and add red chilli powder, black pepper powder, chaat masala and salt to the corn.
  5. Add a few finely chopped coriander leaves and sprinkle some lemon juice. Mix all the ingredients well.
  6. Masala Corn is ready. Serve it when it is warm. You can also prepare this recipe in advance and reheat it slightly at the time of serving.
